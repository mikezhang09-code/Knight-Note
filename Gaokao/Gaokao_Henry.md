## Part III: 2025 New Gaokao Deep Dive & 2026 Strategy

### Context: The 2025 Reformation
2025 marked the first year of the "3+1+2" system in Sichuan. The distinction between Arts and Sciences was abolished, replaced by Physics/History tracks.
*   **Key Change**: "College + Major Group" admission model vs the old "College" line. This causes score segmentation where hot majors (CS/AI) soar while cold majors drop.
*   **Physics+Chemistry Binding**: Almost all top science/engineering majors now require Chemistry, making the "Physics+Chemistry" combination the golden ticket.

### Deep Dive: Only "Rank" Matters
The 2025 "Purification Effect" (e.g., UESTC isolating CS majors) caused cutoff scores to skyrocket.
*   **Rank > Score**: A 650 in 2025 is not the same as 650 in 2026. Always map to provincial rank.
*   **Major Groups**: C9 lowest scores (e.g. ZJU 658) are often for Groups with Agriculture/Ocean. **Do not be misled.** The CS Group will always be Top 300.
*   **Physics+Chemistry**: This combination is essential. 2025 data proves that without Chemistry, access to C9 Tech majors is nearly impossible.

### 2026 Strategy Guide
1.  **Anchor to Rank**:
    *   **Tsinghua/Peking**: Top 80.
    *   **Huadong 5 / UESTC Everest**: Top 500.
    *   **Mid-985 Hot Majors**: Top 1500.
2.  **Strong Base (Qiangji)**:
    *   With Silver Medal entry weakened, **High GK Score + NOIP Background** is the new winning formula.
    *   Focus on USTC/Fudan/Nanjing Math Qiangji if raw score is borderline.
3.  **Application Logic**:
    *   **Aggressive**: Top 10-15 choices for "Mixed Groups" in Top 985 (accepting risk of adjustment).
    *   **Stable**: Middle 15-20 choices for "Pure CS/AI Groups" (e.g., UESTC, Beihang).
    *   **Safety**: Last 10 choices for strong 985s like Sichuan Univ.
